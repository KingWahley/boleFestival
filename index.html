About Bole Festival

In 2016, Bolé Festival lit its first flame — a humble gathering where grilled plantain met pepper sauce, laughter filled the air, and strangers became friends over shared plates. Since then, It's grown into something much bigger.

But here's the thing — it was never just about the food. It's about celebrating who we are. It's about losing your home training for one weekend and remembering what it feels like to live fully — to eat with your hands, dance without shame, laugh till your stomach hurts, and connect with people from all walks of life.
